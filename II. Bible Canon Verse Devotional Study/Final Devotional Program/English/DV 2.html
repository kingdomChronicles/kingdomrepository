Daily Devotional Verses

The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Instruction on how to successfully kickstart your Daily Devotional Lifestyle with the Lord.

  • Start your Daily Devotion by praying sincerely to God, asking Him for understanding and guidance.
  • If you have your own Bible, open it to the verse; otherwise, read the verse from this page.
  • Next, read the devotional verse aloud three times so the enemy can hear God's Word from your lips.
  • Then, read the reflection section multiple times if needed, until you truly understand its message.
  • Then, end your devotion with prayer having a thankful heart.

Bible Verse of the Day

Psalm 119:105

NLT (New Living Translation)

105Your word is a lamp to guide my feet and a light for my path.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


Light is essential for us to navigate in this world considered darkness. Darkness signifies being surrounded by numerous evil things, deeds, and practices. To conquer this darkness, we require light, which is the Word of God, to safely guide us so that we can overcome or avoid the dangers that lie ahead on our path.

In Proverbs, it is written, “But the way of the wicked is like deep darkness; they do not know what makes them stumble.”

We have the choice to walk in darkness or to walk in the light. Yes, there are many dangers along our path, but we can trust that our daily, personal use of God's Word will illuminate those dangers.

In Proverbs, King Solomon shared that the "correction and instruction" found in the lamp of God's Word "is the way of life".

Living in the light of Truth allows God to guide our steps and keep us safe from harm. This is why we are told to always carry the Light and Lamp of God. His Word serves as our light and lamp! It is important to read it daily to ensure we are always guided in our daily journey in this world surrounded by darkness.
