Prayer that touches the heart of God

Congratulations to everyone who has made it this far in your daily devotion.
God now desires to connect with your heart through intimate prayer.
If you follow the instructions provided today, your life and prayer life will be profoundly transformed.
The outcome of these last three days of your devotion will shape your future journey in understanding the ways of the Kingdom of God.

Psalm 55:22

NASB (New New American Standard Bible)

5 Cast your burden upon the Lord and He will sustain you; He will never allow the righteous to be shaken.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


David advises his readers to place their full trust in the Lord. "Cast your burden" means to fully give your worries to God instead of carrying them yourself. God is more capable than any human. When believers release their fears to God, He takes on that burden. This doesn't mean believers are immune to hardship, but it means nothing happens to God's people that isn't for their ultimate good and His glory.



ADDITIONAL INSTRUCTION THAT REQUIRES ACTION:

Seek guidance from God through His Holy Spirit to help you accomplish everything on the list below. Write down all the necessary items for your afternoon or evening devotion.


Simple Prayer for Guidance:

"Lord, help me to follow each step correctly."


Afternoon/Evening Devotion List:

1. List of Individuals Who Have Caused You Pain:

  • Write down the names of all individuals who have caused you pain.

2. List of Individuals You Have Hurt:

  • Record the names of all individuals you have hurt, whether through your words or actions.

3. List of Unkept Promises:

  • List all the promises you have made to anyone but were unable to keep.
  • Ask God for forgiveness during your evening prayer.

4. List of Sins Against God:

  • Write a list of all your sins against God from childhood to the present, whether done in secret or openly.
  • Ask for forgiveness during your evening prayer.

5. List of Other Concerns:

  • Write a list of other concerns you want to include in your prayer.

Instructions:

  • Ensure all necessary lists are completed before starting your evening devotion.
  • Give God ample time during your afternoon/evening devotion, allowing Him to completely renew you today.
