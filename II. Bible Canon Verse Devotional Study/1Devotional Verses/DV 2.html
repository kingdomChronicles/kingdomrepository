Daily Devotional Verses

The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Instruction on how to successfully kickstart your Daily Devotional Lifestyle with the Lord.

  • Start your Daily Verse Devotion by praying earnestly to God that He will give you understanding and guidance in your devotion.
  • If you have your own Bible, open the verse in your bible else, read the verse from this page.
  • Next, read the Devotional Verse 3 times with an audible voice or louder. Let the enemy hear God's Word that is uttered in your lips.
  • Next, read the reflection portion, one or more until the thought of the verse reflection is taken into your heart.
  • Then, end your devotion with prayer having a thankful heart.

Bible Verse of the Day

Psalm 119:105

NLT (New Living Translation)

105Your word is a lamp to guide my feet and a light for my path.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


Light is essential for us to navigate in this world considered darkness. Darkness means we are surrounded by so many evil things, deeds and practices. To overcome this darkness we need light, which is the Word of God, to guide us safely so that we can overcome or avoid the dangers that await on our path.

In Proverbs, “But the way of the wicked is like deep darkness; they do not know what makes them stumble.” We have the choice to walk in darkness or to walk in the light. Yes, there are so many dangers along our path, but we can trust our daily, personal use of God's Word will shine a light on those dangers.

In Proverbs, King Solomon shared that the "correction and instruction" found in the lamp of God's Word "is the way of life".

When we live in the light of Truth, God directs our steps and leads us away from danger. That's why we are instructed to bring always the Light and Lamp of God. His Word is our light and lamp! We must read it every day to guide us always in our daily walk in this world surrounded by darkness.
