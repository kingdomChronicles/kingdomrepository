Daily Devotional Verses

The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Bible Verse of the Day - Morning

Romans 8:31

NLT (New Living Translation)

31 What shall we say about such wonderful things as these? If God is for us, who can ever be against us?


GUIDE REFLECTION:


Many Christians often quote this verse, repeatedly saying that if God is on our side, who can be against us.

These words don't need to be shouted or declared to others as our current state. They are meant only for those who truly stand with the Lord. You might be doing your daily devotions now, but these words may not be for you if your devotion is only in your mind and doesn't reach your heart or manifest in your life. It may be hard to hear, but if you treat the words you read daily as mere entertainment and do not practice them by faith, then you don't have the right to these words. We can better understand this verse if we embrace the words written in Romans 8:1-30, which fully explain who has the right to these verses. Read the given passage in your own copy of the Bible.

If you still find it difficult to follow the Lord, be warned, because it is far more terrifying to be among the Lord's enemies, especially now that we understand the power that God and His army possess. Therefore, we are continually called to remain or claim to be on God's side. For the ones who truly hold to these words are those who will fight for His calling even unto death.
