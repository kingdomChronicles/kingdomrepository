Daily Devotional Verses

The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Instruction on how to successfully kickstart your Daily Devotional Lifestyle with the Lord.

  • Start your Daily Devotion by praying sincerely to God, asking Him for understanding and guidance.
  • If you have your own Bible, open it to the verse; otherwise, read the verse from this page.
  • Next, read the devotional verse aloud three times so the enemy can hear God's Word from your lips.
  • Then, read the reflection section multiple times if needed, until you truly understand its message.
  • Then, end your devotion with prayer having a thankful heart.

Bible Verse of the Day

2 Timothy 3:16-17

NIV (New International Version)

16All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness,17 so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


All Scripture is God's Breathed... All scriptures originate from God and are inspired by the Holy Spirit. While some argue that they are merely written by humans, it is important to recognize that these humans were chosen by God and guided by the Holy Spirit. Scriptures were written to serve as instruction and guidance for humanity.

useful for teaching... The Scripture is beneficial to those who read it, serving as God's way to teach and guide all whom He has called and chosen.

useful for rebuking and correcting... People have many customs, traditions, and practices that we might not be aware of, and these can be sinful acts against God. We can use the scriptures to correct the wrong practices we engage in during our daily lives.

    Sample of practices that God forbids

  • You shall not kill (Exodus 20:13)
  • You shall not steal (Exodus 20:15)
  • Gossip is wrong (Proverbs 20:19)
  • Instruction on excessive eating (Proverbs 23:20-21)
  • Lesson about greed (Luke 12:15)

useful in training in righteousness, So, God wants us to always listen to, read, and meditate His words. This way, we can live a sensible life and stay safe both in our daily lives and after we die.

so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work. This is a message for all servants of God or anyone who wants to become one. We are invited to stay connected to His Word so that we can always be ready to do good things in our daily lives.
