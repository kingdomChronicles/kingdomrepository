Daily Devotional Verses

Prayer that touches the heart of God

Congratulations! You have passed Level 1 of your devotion. Everyone who has reached this far has touched the heart of God through daily devotion.

Now, God wants to have your heart through intimate prayer with Him.

After today's devotion, if you carefully follow all the steps provided, your life and prayer life will never be the same again.

Psalm 55:22

NASB (New New American Standard Bible)

5 Cast your burden upon the Lord and He will sustain you; He will never allow the righteous to be shaken.


INSTRUCTION:


Request guidance from God through His Holy Spirit to help you accomplish all the list stated below. Write down all necessary list needed on your afternoon or evening devotion.

  1. Offer a simple prayer for guidance: "Lord, help me to follow each step correctly."
  2. Make a list of all the individuals who have caused you pain.
  3. Record the names of all individuals you have hurt, whether through your words or actions.
  4. List all the promises you have made to anyone but were unable to keep, and ask God for forgiveness during your prayer time on your evening devotion.
  5. Write a list of all your sins against God from childhood to the present, whether they were done in secret or openly, and ask for forgiveness during your prayer time in the evening.
  6. Write a list of other concerns you want to include on your prayer.
  7. All necessary list should be completed before you start your evening devotion
  8. Give God enough time on your your afternoon/evening devotion, so that God would completely renew you today.
