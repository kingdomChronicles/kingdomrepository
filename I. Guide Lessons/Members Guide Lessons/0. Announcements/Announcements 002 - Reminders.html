III. Mentoring Program

The mentoring program is a Kingdom initiative designed to assist and support the spiritual journey of individuals who are part of the Mountain of God's Glory. We recognize in you the potential and eagerness to learn more about the path of the Kingdom of God. The goal of the mentoring program is to guide those who desire to reach the spiritual level of the Kingdom of God but are struggling to fully understand spiritual truths. Through this guidance, their faith will be strengthened, helping them progress to the Kingdom of God Spiritual level.
