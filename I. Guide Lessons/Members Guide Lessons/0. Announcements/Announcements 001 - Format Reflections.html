Format Reflection for Childrens

For Morning Devotion

Format of Reflection Morning Devotion

Username: Johny
Batch : 16
Location: Manila
Devotion number: Day 25 am
Date: 04/28/24
Time Start: 06:00 am


Reflection:

1. Rewrite the Verse of the day:
Genesis 1:1 - In the beginning, God created the heaven and the earth.

2. Write your prayer concern in the morning:

Dear God our Father,
Thank You for this beautiful morning.
Thank You for waking me up and giving me another day to learn, play, and grow.
Please guide me today, help me be kind, and make good choices.
Watch over my family and friends, and help us feel Your love all day long.
Thank You for always being with me.
I love You, God.
In Jesus Mighty Name.
Amen.



Time End: 06:30 am
