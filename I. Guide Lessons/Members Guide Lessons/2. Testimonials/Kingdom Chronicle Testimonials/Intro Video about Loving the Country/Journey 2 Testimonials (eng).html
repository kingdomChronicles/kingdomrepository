Ages 71 and above

UserName: estrellallarenas
Age: 71
Gender: Female
Location: Cabatuan, Isabela

Message:
RISE, PHILIPPINES...

In our country, there is a lack of unity and concern for others, which is painful to see. It's as if the attitude is "pull your own tail," with little regard for fellow citizens.

In the 1950s and 1960s, the Philippines was one of the wealthiest nations in Asia, known as the "Pearl of the Orient Seas" due to its riches. The term "kayumanggi," which means "pure" and "wise," reflected the nation's esteemed position.

However, it's disheartening to see how our country has fallen. Once admired by many nations, the Philippines is now struggling, losing its dignity. The meaning of "Philippines" has changed to "domestic helper," and we are now one of the poorest countries in Asia, highly polluted and corrupt.

Comparatively, South Korea, once devastated after the war with North Korea, faced extreme poverty and hunger. The Korean president, despite being rejected for loans by other nations, was eventually helped by Germany. With this support, Korea rebuilt, creating factories and jobs. They invested in their people and fostered unity and love for their country.

In contrast, in the Philippines, there is a lack of unity and concern. Filipinos have become self-centered, indifferent to the plight of others, including the less fortunate who beg on the streets, are impoverished, and lack basic necessities.

Where is the "Pearl of the Orient" now, once a rich and esteemed nation in Asia?

Philippines, wake up and rise! Only God can lift our nation from its struggles. He alone knows our origins and our future path. Let us unite, love one another, and return to God, as He promises, "Return to Me and I will return to you... Seek My face and I will heal your land."

If we love God, we should also love our country.

Young people, the hope of our nation, and all Filipinos, let us unite and proclaim God's will to the world through the Kingdom Chronicle.
