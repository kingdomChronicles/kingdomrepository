The Kingdom of God

It’s Essence and it's application

Introduction:
The Gospels say that Jesus preached the message of the Kingdom wherever He went. Therefore, the message about the Kingdom of God is the very essence of Jesus ministry.

The message of the Kingdom of God represents the whole understanding and manifestation of God’s heart for all people, tribes, tongues, and the nations of the world.

The Kingdom of God is not just a component of Christian faith; it is the very foundation of faith.

The one who understands the Kingdom of God possesses the real essence and practice of their faith in God.

Having the right understanding of the Kingdom of God provides a solid foundation about God and will lead to a lifestyle of victorious faith.


I. The Kingdom of God Under the Ruler-ship of Mankind

Dominion over all creation was given to Adam while he walked in close intimacy with God.

1. In Genesis 1 and 2, we see God creating the heavens and the earth. The word used in Genesis 2 for "create" is "bara," which speaks of God’s power to create with just a thought.

  • a. The imagery is of God speaking the waters, stars, and wildlife into existence from His heavenly throne.
  • b. And, if you remember in the creation story, of each thing God creates, He says, “and God saw that it was good”.
  • c. However, the word for "create" used when God creates Adam is different. Here, the word is "yatsar," which means to form or fashion.
    • Unlike creating from His throne in heaven, God is now intimately engaged, like a potter forming a beautiful jar of clay. He breathes life into Adam.
    • And only now does the text say, “and God saw it was very good”.
