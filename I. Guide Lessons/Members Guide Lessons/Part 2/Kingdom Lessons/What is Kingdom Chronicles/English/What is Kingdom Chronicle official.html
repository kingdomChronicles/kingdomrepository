What is Kingdom Chronicle?

A Message to all Filipinoes

Understanding Its Role and Purpose

In a world where spiritual exploration often intertwines with established religious frameworks, Kingdom Chronicle emerges as a unique initiative. It is not a religion, denomination, or sect but rather a conduit designed to bring people closer to God through His Holy Word. The purpose of Kingdom Chronicle is to guide individuals and nations in understanding their divine roles and fulfilling their higher purpose.


What is Kingdom Chronicle?

Kingdom Chronicle is a spiritual journey aimed at connecting people with God in a personal and direct manner. Unlike traditional religious structures, it does not adhere to a specific doctrine or hierarchy but encourages a personal approach to faith. The central belief of Kingdom Chronicle is that individuals can directly receive messages and guidance from God, strengthening their personal relationship with Him through His Word.

At the heart of Kingdom Chronicle is the conviction that every person can experience God’s plan for humanity firsthand. By engaging with God’s Word, participants can gain insights into the divine program intended for all of creation. This understanding provides clarity on why people experience hardships, feelings of inadequacy, and discontentment, offering spiritual enlightenment and solutions to these challenges.


The Purpose of Kingdom Chronicle

The primary goal of Kingdom Chronicle is to call and unite those chosen by God to advance His divine program for humanity.

It aims to:

1. Connect People to God’s Word:

By bringing individuals closer to God through His Holy Word, Kingdom Chronicle seeks to deepen their understanding of spiritual truths and divine purposes.

2. Teach Obedience to God’s Will:

Kingdom Chronicle emphasizes the importance of living in accordance with God’s Word, encouraging individuals to align their lives with divine principles.

3. Foster Personal Divine Relationships:

The initiative encourages a direct and personal relationship between individuals and God, allowing them to receive guidance and understanding of His messages.

4. Reveal God’s Program for Humanity:

Kingdom Chronicle unveils God’s overall plan for His creations, providing insights into the challenges and struggles faced by humanity and ways to overcome them.

5. Highlight the Conflict with the Kingdom of Darkness:

It describes how the Kingdom of Darkness interferes with God’s plan for His creations and offers ways for transformation and restoration of people to God.

6. Promote Unity Among God’s Chosen People:

By gathering God’s chosen people, Kingdom Chronicle aims to unite them in fulfilling God’s mission on Earth.


 Exploring Individual and National Purpose

One of the key aspects of Kingdom Chronicle is exploring purpose at both individual and national levels. It encourages participants to reflect on fundamental questions such as: Why was I created by God, and what is my significant role in my community and the world?

Individual Purpose

1. Why Was I Created as a Human?

Kingdom Chronicle invites individuals to reflect on why they were created and explore the unique purpose and potential bestowed upon them by God.

2. Why Was I Born into My Current Circumstances?

It encourages introspection on why individuals find themselves in specific social or economic conditions, whether wealthy or poor, disabled, or exceptionally talented.

3. Why Am I Here in My Community or Current Job?

The initiative prompts individuals to consider the significance of their current position in their community and work and seek guidance from God for their next steps in alignment with His purpose.

 Understanding one’s individual purpose is the first step toward unlocking a greater sense of meaning and fulfillment in life. By discovering their personal divine role, individuals can align themselves more closely with God’s will and experience true satisfaction.


National and Global Purpose

Once personal purpose is understood, Kingdom Chronicle extends the exploration to national and global levels:

1. What Is My Country’s Purpose?

Participants are encouraged to delve into their nation’s history and role in God’s plan, understanding their country’s mission in the context of the modern world.

2. Who Are We as a Nation?

By examining their identity and origins, participants gain insights into their nation’s purpose and its contribution to global spiritual renewal.

3. The Filipino Diaspora:

For Filipinos scattered around the world, Kingdom Chronicle provides a framework to understand the significance of their global presence and how it aligns with divine purpose.

By aligning personal and national purposes with God’s overarching plan, individuals contribute to the larger mission of establishing God’s kingdom on Earth. This understanding fosters unity, purpose, and fulfillment at both personal and collective levels.


Conclusion

Kingdom Chronicle offers a transformative pathway to understanding and fulfilling divine purpose. By prioritizing a personal relationship with God and exploring the deeper meanings of individual and national existence, it empowers individuals to align their lives with God’s will. Through Kingdom Chronicle, participants can unlock their potential, find true satisfaction, and make meaningful contributions to the divine program for humanity and the world. In these final days, understanding and living one’s divine purpose is more crucial than ever, and Kingdom Chronicle serves as a guiding light on this spiritual journey.
