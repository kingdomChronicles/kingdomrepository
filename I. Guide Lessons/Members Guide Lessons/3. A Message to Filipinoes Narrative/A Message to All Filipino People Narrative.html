Message to All Filipino People

A Message to My Fellow Countrymen


A challenge and an invitation for everyone who reads this message.


Allow me to borrow an essay by a Korean, presented in a unique narrative style, to convey a truth that I wish to share with every Filipino.


MY SHORT ESSAY ABOUT THE PHILIPPINES

by Jaeyoun Kim


Remember this word:

LOVE


Filipinos always complain about the corruption in the Philippines. Do you think the corruption is the problem of the Philippines?


I do not think so.


I strongly believe that the problem is the lack of love for the Philippines


Let me first talk about my country, Korea. It might help you understand my point.


After the Korean War, South Korea was one of the poorest countries in the world.


Koreans had to start from scratch because entire country was destroyed after the Korean War, and we had no natural resources.


Koreans used to talk about the Philippines, for Filipinos were very rich in Asia.


Back then, We envy Filipinos. Koreans really wanted to be well off like Filipinos. We were eager to progress because of the prosperity and development of the Philippines at that time.


Many Koreans died of famine. My father & brother also died because of famine.


Korean government was very corrupt and is still very corrupt beyond your imagination,


but Korea was able to develop dramatically because Koreans really did their best for the common good


with their heart burning with patriotism.

(That generation possessed a great love for their country and fellow Koreans.)


Koreans did not work just for themselves but also for their neighborhood and country.


Education inspired young men with the spirit of patriotism.


40 years ago, President Park took over the government to reform Korea.

(Perhaps over 40 years have passed since this essay was written.)


He tried to borrow Money from other countries, but it was not possible


to get a loan and attract a foreign investment because the economic situation of South Korea was so bad.


Korea had only three factories. So, President Park sent many mine workers and nurses to Germany


so that they could send money to Korea to build a factory.


They had to go through horrible experience.


In 1964, President Park visited Germany to borrow money.


Hundred of Koreans in Germany came to the airport to welcome him and cried there as they saw the President Park.


They asked to him, "President, when can we be well off?" That was the only question everyone asked to him.


President Park cried with them and promised them that Korea would be well off if everyone works hard for Korea,


and the President of Germany got the strong impression on them and lent money to Korea. So, President Park was able to build many factories in Korea.


He always asked Koreans to love their country from their heart.


Many Korean scientists and engineers in the USA came back to Korea to help developing country because they wanted their country to be well off.


Though they received very small salary, they did their best for Korea.


They always hoped that their children would live in well off country.

(As a result, Korea is now much wealthier than the Philippines.)


My parents always brought me to the places where poor and physically handicapped people live.


They wanted me to understand their life and help them.


I also worked at the church while I was in the military. What I learned from the church is that true love for God should reflect in our love for others.


And, I have loved my neighborhood.


I have cried for my country several times. I also cried for the Philippines because of so many poor people.


Have you cried for the Philippines?

(A painful truth: a question about love for the country from someone who is not one of our own.)


I have been to the New Bilibid prison. What made me sad in the prison were the prisoners who do not have any love for their country.


They go to mass and work for Church. They pray everyday. However, they do not love the Philippines.


I talked to two prisoners at the maximum-security compound, and both of them said that they would leave the Philippines right after they are released from the prison.


They said that they would start a new life in other countries and never come back to the Philippines.


Many Koreans have a great love for Korea so that we were able to share our wealth with our neighborhood.


The owners of factory and company were distributed their profit to their employees fairly


so that employees could buy what they needed and saved money for the future and their children.


When I was in Korea , I had a very strong faith and wanted to be a servant of God. However, when I came to the Philippines , I completely lost my faith.


I was very confused when I saw many unbelievable situations in the Philippines.


Street kids always make me sad, and I see them everyday.

(Do we, as citizens of the Philippines, also feel saddened by the state of our country and our less fortunate fellow citizens?)


The Philippines is the only Christian Nation in Asia , but there are too many poor people here.

(Have you ever wondered why, despite knowing that the country is rich in natural resources, there is still so much poverty?)


People go to church every Sunday to pray, but nothing has been changed.

(This is a challenge to everyone, who profess having faith: does your love for God reflect on the way you treat others?)


My parents came to the Philippines last week and saw this situation.


They told me that Korea was much poorer than the present Philippines when they were young. They are so sorry that there are so many beggars and street kids.


When we went to Pagsanjan, I forced my parents to take a boat because it would be fun.


However, they were not happy after taking a boat. They said that they would not take the boat again because they were sympathized the boatmen, for the boatmen were very poor had a small frame.


Most of people just took a boat and enjoyed it. But, my parents did not enjoy it because of love for them.


My mother, who worked at the church since I was young, told me, that if we just go to church without changing ourselves, our type of worship is pure hypocracy.


Faith should come with action. She added that I have to love Filipinos and do good things for them because all of us are same and have received a great love from God.


I want Filipinos to love their neighborhood and country as much as they love God so that the Philippines will be well off.


I am sure that love is the keyword, which Filipinos should remember. We cannot change the sinful structure at once. It should start from person.

(It should start from us)


Love must start in everybody, in a small scale and have to grow.


A lot of things happen if we open up to LOVE.


Let's put away our prejudices and look at our worries with our new eyes. I discover that every person is worthy to be loved.


Trust in love, because. It makes changes possible. Love changes you and me.


Love has the power to change everyone, including you and me.


It transforms people, contexts, and relationships. It changes the world. Please love your neighborhood and country.


Jesus Christ said that whatever we do to others we do to Him.


In the Philippines , there is God for people who are abused and abandoned. There is God who is crying to bestow His love.


If you have a child, teach them how to love the Philippines


Teach them why they have to love their neighborhood and country. You already know that God also will be very happy if you love others.


That's all I really want to ask you Filipinos.

LOVE YOUR COUNTRY.


The statements here are correct, although this is not the exact message of the original text.


I have modified some parts of it to convey the message I want my fellow countrymen to understand.


It's also true that our country was envied by many neighboring countries in the past, and I know that many of my fellow countrymen are still aware of these facts.


A country that was once recognized and admired by the world, now only visible in the annals(record) of history.


Have we ever asked ourselves why this nation has fallen?

(Philippines, how have you lost your brilliance and former glory?)


Have we ever asked ourselves why our fellow countrymen are scattered across different parts of the world?


And many of them, in general saying, are mistreated and belittled by people who belong to other race.


Have we ever asked ourselves why we top the list of news that is not pleasant to people's ears.


Like this one, our country is one of the leading nations in rampant corruption compared to the rest of the world.


And many are aware that corruption is not the only negative label attached to our race and country, even though we are called the only Christian nation in our surrounding countries.


Let’s set aside religion for this matter.

PLEASE,

let us unite as one citizens of our country.


I am just an ordinary citizen, but I want to let every Filipino know that your achievements or status do not matter when it comes to loving your country.


I haven’t achieved much in life and I have nothing to boast about, if the basis is according to the set standards of this world.


Why did I include this topic? Because I want to convey to every Filipino that we have no excuse when it comes to loving our country. Because being a Filipino Race, comes with great responsibility.


I am not as intelligent as well-known intelligent people in our country.


But I believe that a person who loves his country with a passionate heart and has genuine reverence for His Maker can bring change to his country.


This is all I can take pride in: first, the knowledge of God's destiny for my country, if this Nation will take heed on God's voice and claim her destiny for all the people and act on her destiny as a beacon of light for all humanity.


second, my great Love for my Maker.


third, my great Love for my Country.

(Ang Bayan kong Pilipinas)


Let me briefly explain why this message reached you at this moment. Perhaps it was conveyed by people similar of what I believe—some who have little to boast about in life, and others who may have achieved more and are somewhat above others.


But one thing is for sure, we share a common reason, and we both surely possess it:

Our Love for God and Our Love for this Country,

along with the dream that

"This Nation Shall be Great Again".


I, we, and everyone behind this program believe that many have been called by God to advance this advocacy and make it a legacy for all eternity.

We believe you are also called to become part of this advocacy.


I was still at young age when I first heard messages like these. I am grateful to those whom God used to reveal these truths to me, and I know it is also my responsibility to share this truth to many, especially to the people my beloved country.


I tried to share this with many people to the best of my ability, but I knew that my knowledge, skills, and experience were lacking at that time to spread messages like these effectively.


So I prayed to God like this:

"Lord, use me as an instrument for my country,"

and I know this is also the prayer of those people who truly love this country.


That is why this message, has reached you, as an answer to the prayers of yours and many of my fellow countrymen who loves this country so dearly.

We and I, believe that it is a divine appointment for you to hear the truths that change me and gave me the courage to stand for the destiny of this country.


A message from the crying hearts of many, hoping that we can work together as Filipino

To give direction to these goals and aspirations for our country and for the good of all humanity.


A continuous prayerful cry to God, hoping that the citizens of this nation will hear the message from the Creator of the universe about His plans for our country and for all the Nations of the world who will come under His reign and Glory.


A nation appointed by God to stand out in the whole world,

to serve as the beacon of light and hope for all humanity for God's own glory.


A nation where God's presence will dwell until the end and unto eternity.


We believe that the time will come, according to the permission and timing bestowed to us by our Lord God Almighty,


That these message of truth will also reach all the people of my country, with God's voice saying:

"Come, return now to your God, who created you, my Chosen Nation and Country"


And now, with God's help, we wish to convey this message, bearing unique knowledge and wisdom, to share with Filipinos and the entire world the beautiful plan of God for this Country.


A profound history and destiny that everyone should know, messages of truth that will surely be the catalyst of the revival of this race to which you and I belong.


Let us work together to share a beautiful message of truth that every Filipino and all of humanity.


On God's appointed time, we can say to ourselves:

"My beloved Country, Philippines, wait a little longer, and God will restore the glory you once had since the beginning of history."

(A mystery to be unlock to all its people)


A nation once called the Pearl of the Orient Seas.

Wait, and with pride, you will see that no foreign race that once oppressed your children will ever treat you to be at their feet again for God's glory.


The key and promise for the revival of this nation do not lie in people or government, but only in God alone, who has the plan and the power to restore a nation that returns to the feet of her Creator.


God allowed this nation to experience the peak of her suffering, oppression by other races both within and outside the country, calamities, hardships, and other similar afflictions.


God allowed Filipinos to grow weary and disillusioned with the promises of the leaders governing this nation.


So that, in the end, people would return to God, their Creator,

and seek from Him the salvation, restoration and revival of this nation.


And convey to the people that only in Him can they find the answer to salvation and their deliverance from the hands of the oppressive people


and also to convey the message of the long-awaited revival of this nation. The nation of our birth, the land of the Filipinos.

Grandparents, parents, youth, and children, you are being called now to be God's instruments for the rise of this Nation,

The Nation of the Filipino People.


The Nation of His chosen people.
