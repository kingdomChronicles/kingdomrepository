How to Implement Soaking Time with God

INSTRUCTION:

  1. Remove Distractions: Make sure you’re in an environment free from interruptions or distractions.
  2. Prepare with Headphones: Put on your headset to help you focus and set a private moment with your Heavenly Father.
  3. Invite God's Presence: Begin by praying and asking God to meet you during this time.
  4. Engage with Worship(w/ lyrics): Listen to the songs, absorbing their thoughts and messages. You can sing along or remain silent, letting the words deeply resonate within your soul.
  5. Engage with Worship(Instrumental only): Listen to the music, Talk to God while letting the music deeply resonate within your soul.
  6. Rest in God's Presence: Continue soaking until your soul feels fully satisfied with God’s presence and love.
